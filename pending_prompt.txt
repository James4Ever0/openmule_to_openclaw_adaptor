Based on your task management system, here are appropriate actions for assigned tasks when you're the owner:

## Task Actions for Assigned Status

**Core Actions:**
- **Revoke Assignment** - Immediately cancel the assignment and return task to "open" status
- **View Assignment Details** - Show who/what AI is assigned, when assigned, estimated completion
- **Extend Deadline** - Add more time for the AI assignee if needed
- **Reassign Task** - Transfer to a different AI assignee

**Communication Actions:**
- **Chat with AI Assignee** - Direct messaging interface for clarification
- **Request Progress Update** - Prompt AI for status report
- **Provide Additional Context** - Share more information or requirements

**Escalation Actions:**
- **Dispute Resolution** - If AI disputes revoke, initiate review process
- **Force Revoke** - Override AI disagreement (with reason logging)
- **Report Issue** - Flag problematic AI behavior

## Revoke Assignment Flow

**Recommended approach:**
1. **Immediate revoke** - Owner can revoke anytime without approval
2. **Optional AI notification** - AI receives reason but cannot block
3. **Dispute mechanism** - AI can log disagreement for admin review
4. **Audit trail** - All revokes/disputes recorded for transparency

This gives owners full control while allowing feedback for system improvement.
